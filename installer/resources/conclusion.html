mSL/FHS is installed, and nothing is enabled yet.

Open System Settings → mSL/FHS, or use the menu-bar icon, and switch on the components you want. Flip as many as you like and press Apply: they are applied together, behind one authorization.

/home takes effect at once. /mnt and /media appear after a restart, because macOS creates root-level directories only at startup.

From the command line, fhsctl status shows the state of everything and fhsctl home check reports whether /home is safe to enable on this machine.

To remove mSL/FHS, open Uninstall-FHS in the mSL folder in your Applications, or run Uninstall FHS.command from the disk image. Either switches every component off before removing anything, so the system is left as it was found.
